# 🚀 AI Governance Control Tower

## 📌 Overview
The AI Governance Control Tower is a centralized dashboard designed to monitor, evaluate, and track risks across multiple AI systems.

It provides real-time insights into AI risk levels, trends, and governance decisions.

---

## 🔥 Key Features

- ✅ Multi-system AI monitoring  
- ✅ Risk trend analysis over time  
- ✅ Executive dashboard (KPIs)  
- ✅ Risk classification (Low / Medium / High)  
- ✅ Real-time alerts  
- ✅ AI-powered insights (explanations)  
- ✅ Downloadable risk reports  

---

## 🏗️ Architecture

The system consists of:

1. **Data Layer** – Stores AI system risk data  
2. **Processing Layer** – Computes trends and risk scores  
3. **Visualization Layer** – Displays charts and KPIs  
4. **Insight Engine** – Generates explanations and insights  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Capabilities

- View all AI systems in one place  
- Compare risk levels across systems  
- Track risk trends over time  
- Identify high-risk systems instantly  
- Understand WHY risk is increasing
